Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Essence ("the Extension") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the extension and how that information is handled.

Information We Process

Essence processes the visible text content of the webpage that you choose to summarize. This content is used only to generate AI-powered summaries.

Gemini API Key

Essence requires your own Google Gemini API key. Your API key is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and is never transmitted anywhere except directly to Google's Gemini API when you request a summary.

How Website Content Is Used

When you click the Essence extension and request a summary, the visible article text is extracted and securely sent to Google's Gemini API to generate the requested summary. The webpage content is not stored by the extension after processing.

Data Collection

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information.
  • We do not collect browsing history.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect financial information.
  • We do not collect health information.
  • We do not collect personal communications.

Data Sharing

Essence does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. The only information transmitted is the webpage content required to generate summaries through Google's Gemini API after you explicitly request a summary.

Data Security

Your Gemini API key is stored locally within your browser. Essence does not maintain servers or databases containing your information.

Third-Party Services

Essence uses Google's Gemini API to generate summaries. Use of the Gemini API is subject to Google's own privacy policy and terms of service.

Your Choices

You may remove your API key or uninstall the extension at any time. Doing so deletes locally stored extension data from your browser.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
